About Us


A fun narrated sightseeing tour of Fort Lauderdale hosted by one of the most seasoned guides in the industry as he shares with all ages (3-103) the most interesting history, landmarks, waterways, & breathtaking views of Fort Lauderdale & as well as all of its hidden gems.

Who is Event Elevations & Public Relations LLC?

Event Elevations & Public Relations LLC is an event & public relations firm that handles a variety of events & activities both large & small from in store sampling to national experiential marketing; they provide event staff, MCs, DJs as well as strategize innovative branding concepts while continuously improving existing brand concepts.

Who is Howie The Tour Guide?

Howie The Tour Guide or just "Howie" has been & is an ambassador, tour guide as well as a liaison between the public & many local small businesses all over Florida including The Florida keys, Dade County, Broward County, Palm Beach County & (most importantly for this tour activity) Fort Lauderdale since 2008. Prior to 2008 Howie had a respectable amount of public relations experience & this can be found in Howie's Bio here________________.
