Our Mission

A fun narrated sightseeing tour of Fort Lauderdale hosted by one of the most seasoned guides in the industry as he shares with all ages (3-103) the most interesting history, landmarks, waterways, & breathtaking views of Fort Lauderdale & as well as all of its hidden gems.

Partners 

How We See The City! or Howie - See The City!" is a concept created by Event Elevations and Public Relations LLC in coordination with Funky Fish Shuttles and Tours; "How We See The City! or Howie - See The City!" is hosted by Howie The Tour Guide. Who is Funky Fish Shuttles and Tours? Funky Fish Shuttles and Tours is a South Florida based transportation provider that is (in our opinion) definitely is the best way to travel around Fort Lauderdale and handles all your local transportation needs while at the same time gives its guests an opportunity to enjoy the beautiful outdoor atmosphere that only South Florida can provide. Funky Fish Shuttles and Tours is available for all kinds of outdoor outings and would be a great addition to any activity you are thinking about doing throughout the City.
